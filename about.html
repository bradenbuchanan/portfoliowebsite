Meet Braden

I am an accomplished UX Research Lead with a passion for improving the user experience. With extensive experience in research and design, Braden has a proven track record of leading successful user-centered projects in a variety of industries.

Currently serving as a UX Research Lead at Roche Diabetes Care, Braden is responsible for conducting research and analysis to help inform product design decisions. He is a skilled communicator and collaborator, working closely with cross-functional teams to develop innovative solutions that meet both user and business needs. Prior to joining Roche Diabetes Care, Braden worked at several top-tier companies, including Amazon and Google, where he honed his skills in research, design, and project management. I holds a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science Indiana University-Purdue University Indianpolis.

In my freetime, I enjoy exploring new hiking trails, podcasting, reading, snowboarding, and working out.
